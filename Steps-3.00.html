3. How to create Tag table?

In this step we have created a Job tag  and attached the tag information with the  dbo. CB_MKTG_JOBS table. By doing this any update  or insertion on dbo. CB_MKTG_JOBS will be populated on the dimension that we are created. The script attached is  a process or act of  attaching  a label to something. In this process the job tags is used to attach the label of the job positions. 

This process performs a while loop on the job description to find the  the matching job tag and loaded them to  the job tag table.
